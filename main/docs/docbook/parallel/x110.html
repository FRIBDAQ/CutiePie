3.2. Parallel DDASSpecTcl

Example 3-6. Obtaining the Parallel SpecTcl skeleton

	    mkdir parallel
	    cd parallel
	    cp /usr/opt/spectcl/6.0-000/DDASSkel/* .

The skeleton consists of several files; Makefile and MySpecTcl.cpp, CMyProcessor.cpp (which is what you want to modify for your needs) and other files that you won't need to edit.
